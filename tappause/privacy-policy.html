Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-01-23

1. Introduction

Welcome to TapPause. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. TapPause operates entirely offline on your device. All sounds, timers, and settings are stored locally on your device and never leave your device.

3. Data Storage

All app data, including timer settings, preferences, sound preferences, and app settings are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

TapPause does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not integrate with any external services that would collect your data.

5. Children's Privacy

TapPause is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data, there are no privacy concerns for children using this app.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@louisbuilds.me.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. All data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
