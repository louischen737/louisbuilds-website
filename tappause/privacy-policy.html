Privacy Policy for TapPause

Last Updated: 2026-01-23

1. Introduction

TapPause is a simple pause timer designed to be quiet, intentional, and distraction-free. This Privacy Policy explains how TapPause handles information when you use the app.

2. Information We Collect

TapPause does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely offline on your device. All timers, sounds, and settings remain on your device and never leave it.

3. Data Storage

All app data, including pause duration, preferences, sound settings, and other configurations, are stored locally on your device using iOS system storage (UserDefaults). This data is not accessible to us and is never transmitted externally.

4. Third-Party Services

TapPause does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

5. Children's Privacy

TapPause is suitable for users of all ages. Because the app does not collect any personal information, there are no data-related privacy concerns for children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or TapPause, you can contact us at support@louisbuilds.me.

8. Your Rights

Because TapPause does not collect or process personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. Removing the app from your device deletes all locally stored data.
