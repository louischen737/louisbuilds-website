Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 22, 2025

Welcome

PurrrrrFocus (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "this app") takes your privacy protection very seriously. This privacy policy details how we protect your personal information. By using this app, you agree to the practices described in this privacy policy.

Information Collection

This app is designed with complete local storage and does not collect any personally identifiable information:

  1. Local Data: Tasks you create, focus records, settings preferences, etc., are stored only locally on your device.
  2. No Data Collection: The app does not collect any usage statistics or analytics data.
  3. No Network Transmission: All data remains on your device and is never uploaded to any servers or third parties.
  4. In-App Purchases: The app provides Plus version in-app purchase functionality. Payment processing is handled by Apple App Store, and we do not collect or store your payment information.

Data Usage

Since the app uses complete local storage, we do not collect any information. App functionality is entirely based on local data on your device:

  • Provide app functionality: Based on local data on your device
  • Provide customer support: Through in-app feedback feature
  • Ensure app security and stability: Through iOS system security mechanisms
  • Comply with legal obligations: Follow relevant laws and regulations

Note: All data processing occurs on your device and is never uploaded to any servers.

Data Storage

  • Local Database: Uses iOS CoreData framework to store your focus records, task data, etc.
  • System Settings: Uses iOS UserDefaults to store app settings and preferences
  • Device Local: All data is stored only on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: Data is not synced to iCloud or other cloud services
  • Data Export: Plus version supports exporting data in CSV/Excel format, files stored in device local temporary directory
  • Data Deletion: You can delete local data at any time through in-app settings

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Since the app uses complete local storage, we may only share information in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements or court orders
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

User-Initiated Sharing Features:

  • Data Export Feature: Plus version supports exporting your focus data in CSV/Excel format. Exported files are stored in device local temporary directory, and you can choose to share the data to other apps through system sharing functionality
  • App Sharing Feature: You can share app recommendations to other users. Shared content includes App Store links and does not contain personal data
  • Achievement Card Sharing: You can share achievement unlock information to social media platforms. Shared content includes achievement names and descriptions, but does not contain personal statistics
  • Achievement Image Saving: You can save achievement cards to device photo album. Images contain achievement names and descriptions, but do not contain personal statistics

Note: Apart from the above user-initiated sharing behaviors, we do not automatically share your data. The use of data after sharing is controlled by you and the recipients, and we do not participate in it.

Data Security

We take appropriate technical measures to protect your personal information:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored only on your device and never uploaded to any server
  • System Protection: Data is protected by iOS system security mechanisms
  • Data Isolation: App data is completely isolated from other app data
  • No Network Transmission: All data is never transmitted over the network

Your Rights

Since this app uses complete local storage, you have full control over your data:

  • Data Access: All data is stored on your device and you can view it anytime
  • Data Modification: You can modify or delete any data at any time
  • Data Export: Plus version supports exporting focus statistics data in CSV/Excel format
  • Data Reset: You can reset all app data at any time
  • Data Deletion: Deleting the app completely removes all data

This app does not require an account deletion feature as there are no server-side accounts.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications or update logs. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through:

  • Email: support@louisbuilds.me
  • In-app feedback: Through the feedback feature in the app settings page

We will respond to your inquiry promptly.
